Projects

Climate Controlled Smart House

This was my grad project in my final year of Computer Engineering. The task was to build any product or prototype that utilized all the skills gained throughout the program. I came up with idea for a smart house which can have different temperatures in different rooms inside 1 house. The temperature sensors were wireless, sending signal back to the micro controller, which had a GUI built from Java to control specific temperature zones. The heater apparatus had dampers inside of the ducts which would open and close depending on the temperature reading.
